Ultrasonic robot reference notes

Pins used by the sketch:
- Trig: pin 2
- Echo: pin 3
- Left motor direction: pins 4 and 5
- Left motor speed/PWM: pin 6
- Right motor direction: pins 7 and 8
- Right motor speed/PWM: pin 9

Distance formula:
- pulseIn(Echo, HIGH) returns the echo time in microseconds.
- Speed of sound is about 340 m/s, or 0.034 cm/us.
- The sound travels to the object and back, so divide by 2.
- Simplified distance in centimeters: cm = echoTime * 17 / 1000.

Behavior idea:
- If the measured distance is below the threshold, stop or reverse, then turn.
- If the measured distance is above the threshold, keep driving forward.

Safety:
- Test with wheels lifted first.
- Use a motor driver; do not connect DC motors directly to Arduino pins.
